Excercise 24 An apple for the thirst


For this excercise we need to make a SVG in D3 with a array of 20 objects that are created dynamic.

With the data of those objects we create 20 circles in the SVG random with several colors.


Info:

-To create the An apple for the thirst SVG press the button below.

-When you press the button again it re-creates 20 circles on a other spot and with a other color.

-To remove a certain circle you need to press on the circle what you want to remove.
